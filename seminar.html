Internet of Things (IOT)

Brief History and Evolution:

- TCP/IP Protocols: (1980s - 1990s)
- Kevin Ashton coined the term "IOT"

How IOT Works:

+ Smartphone: - Sensor
- Platforms e.g. COAP
- Applications
- Devices
- Connections e.g. NB-IOT, IOWPAN, Sigfox, NFC, WiSun, LTE, LoRaWAN

IOT in the Real World:

- Smart home use: e.g. smoke alarm sensor, doorbell sensor, water leakage sensor, etc.

Challenges of IOT:

- Cost
- Connectivity issues
- Security
- Compatible devices
